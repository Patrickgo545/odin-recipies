Lumpia

Ingredients

First things first.. its way too much work to make these things from scratch

Do what everyone else does & cheat. Buy it from your local Filipino market. You'll thank me later

  • Pre-made lumpia rolls (from market)
  • BOOM! easy

Cooking instructions

  1. Fill a skillet with oil up to 1/2"
  2. Bring oil to high temperature
  3. Lay lumpia rolls on skillet (approx. 2 min each side)
  4. Pull rolls off skillet once every side is crispy brown
  5. Serve to your guests. Make sure they know that you made these delicious rolls by scratch & you love them that much
